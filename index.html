Locations

Vista

Vista, known as "America's Climatic Wonderland," enjoys a Mediterranean climate, characterized by mild, wet winters and warm, dry summers. Its location just 7 miles inland from the Pacific Ocean contributes to its moderate weather, making it an attractive place for those who enjoy outdoor activities year-round.

Encinitas

Encinitas, nestled along the coast in San Diego County, is celebrated for its natural beauty and mild Mediterranean climate. With temperatures rarely dropping below 40°F or rising above 85°F, Encinitas offers an idyllic setting for beach activities, gardening, and surfing, complemented by breathtaking coastal vistas and lush landscapes.
